SELENA GOMEZ

Selena Gomez was known for her starring role on the 2007–12 Disney television series Wizards of Waverly Place and as the singer of such pop hit songs as “Come & Get It” and “Same Old Love.American actress and singer who won legions of young fans as the winsome star of the Disney television series Wizards of Waverly Place (2007–12) and as a pop vocalist.

Selena Gomez, who grew up in a suburb of Dallas, began her acting career in 2002 as a child on the PBS children’s TV series Barney & Friends. She began her music career on Disney projects, including singing the theme song for Wizards of Waverly Place, in which she also starred.
